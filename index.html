// World Setting

The Ruins of Oni-Zakura

At the end of the Warring States era, a once-mighty fortress was consumed by flames of war. Now it exists as a cursed space frozen in time — a ruined castle where petals of pink and violet fall eternally beneath a twilight sky.

The protagonist, Onimaru (鬼丸) — "Blade of the Demon" — was a samurai who fell to a terrible curse. His body now bears a demon's mask, twin horns, and eyes burning with crimson flame. He does not remember his past. He only knows one truth: as long as the curse remains unbroken, he must fight.

Every enemy slain releases a fragment of purified curse energy. Every coin collected makes him stronger. And somewhere, beyond ten thousand kills, the mask may finally shatter.

"Until this flame burns away every last curse — I will not let go of this blade."

— Onimaru 鬼丸
// Laws of This World
  • The ruined castle has no end — enemies pour in from both sides, forever.
  • Enemies are born from the curse — soldiers' wraiths, phantom archers, and towering Oni.
  • Cherry blossoms are both beauty and curse — enemies dissolve into petals when slain.
  • Sake is the key to Awakening — the cursed liquor ignites the demon's full power.
  • Coins are crystallized curse energy — collect them, grow stronger, fight longer.
  • The curse can be broken — but only by one who refuses to stop.
// The Demon Samurai

Onimaru — Two States of Fury

Normal State

通常 · TSŪJŌ

Dark armor and a dim crimson cape. Eyes glow quietly behind the demon mask. Onimaru moves with the disciplined ferocity of a seasoned swordsman — each strike precise, each step deliberate. His katana cleaves through wraiths with fluid, practiced aggression.

WeaponStandard Katana
AttackSingle-direction slash combo chain
StyleFast, controlled, technical

Awakening State

覚醒 · KAKUSEI — Flaming Sword

When the Awakening Gauge hits MAX, the curse ignites. Onimaru's entire body erupts in flame. His eyes blaze with blinding light. The katana becomes a flaming blade of devastating power — strikes are wider, pierce through multiple enemies, and leave trails of fire that purify all they touch.

TriggerAwakening Gauge reaches 100 (Sake + kills)
AttackFLAME SLASH — wide arc, piercing
Duration8s base → up to 20s (fully upgraded)
// Those Who Stand in the Way

Enemies of the Cursed Ruin

👤

Wraith Soldiers

亡魂 · MUKURO

The restless souls of soldiers who died in the castle's fall. They close in from both sides, relentless and numerous. The backbone of every wave — underestimate them at your peril.

🏹

Phantom Archers

幽霊弓手 · YŪREI YUMITE

Spectral bowmen who fire from range. Their arrows cannot be blocked — only jumped over. Three arrow types: green (slow), blue (medium), red (fast). Stay airborne or stay dead.

👹

The Oni

鬼 · ONI

Massive demon lords with enormous HP pools. They attack up close and hurl boulders from range. Defeating one drops a golden coin worth 20 — the greatest challenge, the greatest reward.

// Core Systems

How You Fight to Survive

01 //

Free Movement

Drag anywhere on the left 40% of the screen to move freely left and right. Chase coins, dodge enemies, position for the perfect strike — full tactical freedom in a 2D plane.

02 //

Jump + Attack

Two dedicated buttons on the right side. Jump to dodge arrows. Attack to slash enemies. Combine them mid-air for a devastating Jump Attack (落下斬) that craters enemies below.

03 //

Combo Chain

Each successive kill within the combo window multiplies your score up to ×5.0. Build rhythm: move, slash, jump, slash again. The combo is your momentum — don't let it drop.

04 //

Awakening Gauge

Fill the gauge by killing enemies and collecting Sake items (+50 each). At MAX (100), Awakening triggers automatically — an unstoppable 8–20 second rampage of flame and ruin.

05 //

Coin Upgrades

Every slain enemy drops coins. Spend them in the Upgrade Shop between runs: boost HP, attack power, awakening duration, coin magnet range, and coin bonus multiplier. Get stronger every session.

06 //

Kill Record

Your score is your kill count. Beat your personal best. Every new record is proof that you have grown — that the curse of yesterday's Onimaru has been surpassed.

// Aesthetic Philosophy

Three Pillars of the World

幽玄 · YŪGEN

Profound Beauty

Beauty that lies beyond what is visible

Mist-shrouded ruins, distant silhouettes of shattered towers, petals that fall without wind. The world of ONIZAKURA is beautiful precisely because it is broken and cursed. Every visual speaks of something lost.

無常 · MUJŌ

Impermanence

All things pass — and that is why they matter

Cherry blossoms are beautiful because they fall. Game over is not failure — it is part of the cycle. Every run ends. Every run teaches. Rise, and the next run begins with the wisdom of the last.

克己 · KOKKI

Self-Mastery

The true enemy is within

The wraiths and archers are projections. The real challenge is the urge to quit after a bad run. Each retry is an act of will. Each new record is evidence that you have conquered your previous self.
